About me

Design isn't decoration.
It's how things work.

I'm a UI designer who built my experience at a digital agency working across a range of ecommerce clients — product pages, checkout flows, landing pages, and everything in between. I've seen firsthand what makes people click, scroll, and convert, and what makes them leave.

I'm now stepping into freelance for the first time, bringing that same commercial eye to projects outside the agency world. If you need a sharper, cleaner interface or a redesign that goes beyond a fresh coat of paint — let's talk.

UI Design

  • High-fidelity UI design

Redesign & Refresh

Got a page that's not converting, or an interface that just feels dated? I'll take a hard look at what's not working and redesign it with usability and results in mind — not just aesthetics.

  • Mobile-first optimization
  • Ecommerce page redesign

Frontend Implementation

Need the designs brought to life? I can translate Figma or Sketch files into clean, responsive HTML and CSS — great for small teams who want one person to handle design and build.

  • UI polish & refinement
